  • Novel Methodology to Improve Out-of-scope Accuracy
    To build a robust task-oriented dialogue system, it is important to have good accuracy for both in-scope intent classification and also to identify those queries that are out-of-scope. Consider a chatbot for a banking application that interacts with customers, identifies the intent of their queries and redirects them to the appropriate representatives. If such a system cannot correctly identify out-of-scope queries, it could waste significant amount of time for both the customers and the banking representatives due to wrong routing. Previous work by Stefan et. al, 2019 clearly showcase that when state-of-the art NLP models are used for intent classification, they suffer from poor out-of-scope accuracy. We propose a novel methodology to augment an existing domain specific dataset with out-of-scope queries using a generative NLP language model. We show that our methodology can achieve up to 1.8X improvement in out of scope accuracy.
